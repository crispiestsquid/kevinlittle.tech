earth invaders

This was a game that I made in my MULT 210 class back at CCBC Essex. It was our final game project for the SDE program there. We had to team up into groups of two, and show how much we had learned from all of our game programming classes. I did all the programming, and put everything together in the .fla file. Steve Hessenauer was my partner, and man could he do some art! This was one of my proudest moments as a game dev. We never got around to 100% but it runs well as a demo.
